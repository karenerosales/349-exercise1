Apple Pie


Recipe Details

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 40 minutes

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 package premade Pie Crust.
  • 6 cups thinly sliced Peeled Apples
  • 3/4 cups Sugar
  • 2 tablespoons All Purpose Flour
  • 3/4 teaspoons Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon Ground Nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F.
  2. Place pie crust in oven.
  3. Mix ingredients in seperate bowl and put into pie crust.
  4. Place bottom layer under top layer and seal edges together.
  5. Bake 40-45 minutes or until apples tender.

Tip

Ice cream makes a great addition.
